Himalayas

The Himalayas are the world's highest mountain range, stretching across Asia and forming a natural barrier along the northern border of India, encompassing several countries like Nepal, Bhutan, and China; with Mount Everest, the highest peak globally, situated within its range; considered young fold mountains, the Himalayas are divided into different parallel ranges including the Great Himalayas (Himadri), Lesser Himalayas (Himachal), and the Shiwaliks, with significant rivers like the Ganga and Indus originating from its glaciers;.
